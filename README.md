# ENDevo Inc.

**Death Tech infrastructure for digital legacy, end-of-life readiness, and workforce continuity.**

160,000 people die every day. Every one of them leaves behind digital accounts, assets, obligations, and unfinished decisions. Most employers, families, and institutions are unprepared for what happens next.

ENDevo builds the readiness layer: assessment, planning, and continuity tools for individuals and the organizations that employ them.

---

### What we're building

| Product | Stage | What it does |
|---|---|---|
| **Jesse** | v1 → SaaS | AI-guided digital readiness scanner. Identifies gaps in digital legacy, access, and continuity. |
| **Q4 → Q40** | In development | Tiered readiness assessment. From 4-question screen to 40-question enterprise audit. Powers the ENDevo Readiness Score. |

More in the pipeline. This org houses the codebase across products.

---

### Who we serve

- **Enterprise:** HR, benefits, EAP, and risk leaders managing workforce continuity exposure.
- **Individuals:** Anyone who wants their digital life to be navigable by the people they love.
